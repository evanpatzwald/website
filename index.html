Episode Review: Help Wanted

Release Date: May 1, 1999

THE GOOD: This episode is pretty funny, especially being only the beginning of the SpongeBob SquarePants series. Quite a few jokes hit hard, and it provides the memorable “Living in the Sunlight, Loving in the Moonlight” sequence we all know and love. This episode clearly shows that even when SpongeBob was just getting started, it was going to be a great show, eventually leading to the yellow Sponge we all know and love. It sets up the characters well, and does a good job explaining the premise of the show. “Help Wanted” serves as a good episode to introduce anyone to SpongeBob on.

THE BAD: Not too much is bad with “Help Wanted”, though it does still have its faults. A few gags, particularly the one where SpongeBob trips on a nail, are a little too long, and there is some pacing issues here and there. However, since this is the first episode of the show, these issues can be mostly forgiven, and overall, “Help Wanted” is a great episode of SpongeBob everyone looking for a good SpongeBob episode should check out.

Overall: 8/10
